--- title: "A Published Essay on the Commercial Press of China" excerpt: "Yes, I did humanitarian researches." collection: portfolio --- In honor of the founder of Fudan University, Priest Xiangbo Ma, I wrote an essay named《浅论近代商务印书馆的经营之道》, which investigated in the history of The Commercial Press of China and its relation of the university's founder. The essay is collected and published in a book named 《博学杯·2016—2017：海上的记忆与寻踪》, via Fudan University Press.
